Privacy Policy for Network Analyzer

Last Updated: 2024

1. Introduction

Network Analyzer ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") handles information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be absolutely clear: Network Analyzer does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal user data.

3. Permissions Required and Why

3.1 READ_PHONE_STATE Permission

Purpose: This permission is required to:

  • Read network type (2G, 3G, 4G, 5G, Wi-Fi)
  • Monitor signal strength (RSRP, RSRQ, RSSI)
  • Detect network operator information
  • Access telephony state for network analysis

What we access: Only technical network data. We do NOT access:

  • Phone numbers
  • Call logs
  • Text messages (SMS/MMS)
  • Contact information
  • Device IMEI or personal identifiers

3.2 ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION Permission

Purpose: Required to access cell tower information for accurate signal strength measurement.

What we access: Cell tower location data only. We do NOT track your GPS location.

3.3 INTERNET Permission

Purpose: Required for internet speed testing functionality.

4. Data Processing and Storage

  • ✅ All data processing happens locally on your device
  • ✅ No data transmission to our servers or third parties
  • ✅ No cloud storage of any information
  • ✅ No user accounts required
  • ✅ No advertising or analytics tracking

5. Third-Party Services

Network Analyzer does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
